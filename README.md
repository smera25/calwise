# 🥗 CalWise – AI-Powered Nutrition & Wellness Platform

CalWise is a full-stack health and nutrition management platform designed to help users make smarter dietary decisions through personalized meal planning, nutritional analysis, health analytics, and community-driven wellness engagement.

The platform combines intelligent nutrition tracking with data-driven insights to create a holistic health management experience that promotes healthier lifestyle choices and long-term wellness goals.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

Maintaining a balanced diet and healthy lifestyle can be challenging due to the overwhelming amount of nutritional information available today.

CalWise addresses this challenge by providing users with a centralized platform to:

* Track nutritional intake
* Generate personalized meal plans
* Explore healthy recipes
* Monitor health progress
* Analyze dietary habits
* Engage with a wellness-focused community

The system delivers actionable insights that help users make informed nutritional decisions based on their goals and preferences.
